Buttons

One primary per screen. Primary = orange fill, Secondary = white with gray border, Ghost for toolbar actions. Destructive primary keeps the orange fill — intent lives in the verb ("删除", "移除并继续"), never in a red fill button.

Variants
Sizes
With icon
Use a single orange primary as the strongest action on the screen. Everything else steps down to secondary or ghost.
Don't stack two orange primaries next to each other — pick one. Don't use a red fill for "delete"; keep the orange and change the verb.

Inputs & forms

Label-left layout. Labels are 120 px wide, right-aligned, so wet text lines up with the field baseline. Required fields carry a red asterisk before the label — never the word "必填".
